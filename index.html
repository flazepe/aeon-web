Aeon

Aeon is a Discord bot that is focused on simplicity. Despite it being minimalist, it offers various useful features that you can use on your servers.

Features

Moderation

Moderation features such as purge, kick and ban are available.

Language

Translator and Dictionary are included.

Fun

Variety of fun features such as InspiroBot, xkcd, and GIF are inside Aeon for you to have a good laugh.

Development

Development features such as DNS and IP Lookup exist for developers to use.
